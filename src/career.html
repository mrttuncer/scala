Introduction

At Tricon, we credit our success and rapid growth to our greatest asset: Our People.

Tricon has grown from a small company of 4 employees in 1996 to nearly 500 today. Our highly motivated and success-driven teams have been able to thrive in our entrepreneurial environment. We at Tricon set high standards for ourselves, but believe our commitment to success is what sets us apart in the industry. Our culture is one of great autonomy, allowing employees to find creative solutions and make decisions, enabling professional growth.

At Tricon, one's career is what he or she makes of it through hard work and perseverance. Our roles are designed to provide our people with unlimited learning opportunities and exposure to the entire trade life cycle. This allows for better understanding of the business and, in turn, exponential career development. Read through some of our employees' stories to see what they have achieved at our organization.

Working with passionate and experienced colleagues at Scala is rewarding and key to becoming front runner in a business that we developed from scratch. The varierty of chemical products that we trade at Scala is an important factor to develop synergies, be global, bring more added value to our counterparties and participate in cost saving in the supply chain process.
