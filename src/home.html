Adding Value Into the Petro Chemical Chain

Scala’s objective is to deliver complete customer satisfaction by addressing the main needs od the physical commodity industry, by continually providing an added value to the overall supply chain.

Scala is an industry leader in the global trade and distribution market.

Logistics

Logistics management is a key activity and core competence in our business. Our expertise covers multiple modes of transport, including railcars, barges, and both dry bulk and liquids vessels. We recently created Lighthouse, a freight brokerage joint venture, continuing to strengthen our capabilities in this critical area.

Risk Management

We manage various risks in our operations, including price risk, credit, liquidity and operational risks. The goal of our process is to minimize costs for our suppliers and customers, while reducing uncertainty inherent in the commodity trade flows we handle.

Market Intelligence

Our extended coverage around the world, with presence across numerous markets provides us with unique insights and allows us to spot valuable opportunities. We carefully cultivate relationships with market participants, including via sponsorship and participation of several industry conferences.

Financing

Inherent to our business we provide various forms of financing to our customers and suppliers. We participate in prepayment arrangements, secured financing structures, and provide open account terms.

Career

what it's like to be part of the Scala team

Fundamental to the growth of the business is our ability to attract, hire, and retain the best talent. Our people are among the most creative, ambitious and diverse in the industry, and we constantly strive to build on our exceptional talent.

Sustainable Development

Our Mission

We aim for our activities to contribute to a more just, equal, and inclusive society, where we continuously improve our environmental impact on the journey to a net zero, circular economy.
