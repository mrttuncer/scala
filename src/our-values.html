Who We Are?

Our Values

Tricon’s success and leadership has been achieved by remaining loyal to the pillars on which the Company was founded.

Our Core Principles

Scala strives for reliability of service and competitiveness in our proposals, in turn generating the greatest possible results for our partners and shareholders.

We believe the following values set us apart from our competition:

  • Leadership - Inspiring others to engage with our vision.
  • Passion - Expressing enthusiasm in our daily work.
  • Innovation - Thinking outside the box to add value throughout the supply chain.
  • Strong work ethic - Possessing a desire to work hard and willingness to put in the extra effort to get the job done.
  • Perseverance - Commitment to not give up when faced with adversity or challenge.
  • Teamwork - Demonstrating commitment to one another as well as those with whom we conduct business.

We expect our employees and business partners to live these values by conducting their work ethically and encourage anyone to report concerns about our business conduct to the Scala Listens Helpline.

diversity

The diversity of our people is one of our greatest strengths.

Scala employs more than 400 employees globally with diverse cultures and backgrounds. As a global supplier and purchaser of products worldwide, Scala operates across a range of cultures and international markets and is committed to working well with all the communities with which we conduct business.

Scala’s objective is to deliver complete customer satisfaction by addressing the main needs of the physical commodity industry, by continually providing an added value to the overall supply chain.
